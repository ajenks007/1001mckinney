An Iconic Houston Masterpiece with Modern Amenities

Designed by famous Houston architect Alfred C. Finn in 1947, 1001 McKinney is one of only two office buildings in downtown Houston listed on the National Register of Historic Places. A recently completed renovation restored the downtown high rise to its former glory, complete with secure key card access, 24/7 security, an on-site fitness center, tenant lounge, conference room, and more.
