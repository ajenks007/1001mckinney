History

A Houston Landmark, Fully Renewed

Designed by Alfred C. Finn and completed October 13, 1947, 1001 McKinney is listed on the National Register of Historic Places. In 2020, TRC Capital Partners engaged Gensler to restore the Art Deco lobby to its original grandeur while adding all modern conveniences.
