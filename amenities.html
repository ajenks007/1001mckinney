What We Offer

World-Class Amenities in a Historic Setting

From the moment you arrive to the moment you leave, every detail at 1001 McKinney is designed to make your workday exceptional.

On-Site Fitness Center

McKinney Fitness offers treadmills, weight machines, showers, and a private dressing room with towel service, steps from your office.

Tenant Lounge and Coffee

The 5th floor Tenant Lounge features a premium gourmet coffee machine available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, free of charge to all tenants.

777-Space Parking Garage

Directly connected via a 6th floor skybridge with keyless entry. Reserved and covered spaces available. Managed by Winpark Management.

24/7 Secure Keycard Access

Lobby Ambassadors on staff around the clock, led by Security Director Officer Albert Jones. Your team and your assets are always protected.

Downtown Tunnel Access

Direct elevator and escalator access to 95 blocks of Houston's underground tunnel system with nearly 400,000 sq ft of retail and dining.

Conference Facility

A boardroom-quality conference center with reception and catering options, available to all tenants for presentations and client meetings.

Full-Service Car Detailing

A full-service car detailer operates directly in the parking garage, so your vehicle is cared for while you focus on business.

On-Site Retail Services

A bank, jeweler, printer, and hairstylist are all located in the tunnel-level retail: 11,156 sq ft of convenience below the building.

Morton's The Steakhouse

Fine dining with a speakeasy-like atmosphere, right in the building lobby. Business lunches, client dinners, and after-work celebrations alike.

2020 Renovation

Six Modernized Elevators

All six elevators were fully modernized in 2020. Press the antique-brass call button and arrive in style, a perfect blend of historic character and modern efficiency that greets your team and clients every single day.

The renovation, led by Gensler Houston, also introduced a new canopy over the McKinney Street entrance, additional lobby windows, custom rugs, new furniture, and upgraded finishes throughout all common corridors.
