Building Amenities

1001 McKinney has retained its pristine historic architecture while seamlessly incorporating ultra-modern finishes throughout, including secure keycard access, an on-site gym, tenants lounge and more.

World-Class Amenities in a Historic Setting

From the moment you arrive to the moment you leave, every detail at 1001 McKinney is designed to make your workday exceptional.

🏋 On-Site Fitness Center

State-of-the-art treadmills, weight machines, and a private dressing room with towel service — all just steps from your office.

☕ Tenant Lounge & Coffee

The 5th floor Tenant Lounge offers a premium gourmet coffee machine available 24/7, providing custom cappuccinos and espresso at no charge.

🚗 777-Space Parking Garage

Directly attached to the building with keyless entry, reserved spaces available. Enter through the 5th floor breezeway — no more walking in the rain.

🔓 24/7 Secure Access

Secure keycard access to the building around the clock, with 24/7 on-site security ensuring your team and assets are always protected.

🛒 Downtown Tunnel Access

Direct access to Houston's underground tunnel system — connecting you to over 95 blocks of shops, restaurants, and services without stepping outside.

🛠 Conference Room

A beautifully appointed conference room available to all tenants, perfect for client presentations, team meetings, and important events.

🚗 Full-Service Car Detailing

A full-service car detailer operates directly in the parking garage, so your vehicle is fresh and clean while you focus on business.

📦 On-Site Services

Printer, shoe shine, bank, and jeweler all conveniently on-site — everything you need without ever leaving the building.

🍽 Morton's The Steakhouse

Houston's premier steakhouse is located right in your building lobby — perfect for business lunches, client dinners, or a celebratory evening.

Six Modernized Elevators

Six elevators, all modernized in 2020, whisk you effortlessly between floors. Press the antique-brass call button and arrive in style — a perfect blend of historic character and modern efficiency.
